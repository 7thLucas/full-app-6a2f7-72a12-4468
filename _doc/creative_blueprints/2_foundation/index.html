Marketing agencies manage reviews across email threads, Slack, and shared drives — with no structure, no version control, and no audit trail.

Feedback gets lost
Scattered across threads, no single source of truth
Wrong versions approved

No approval record
Who approved what, when? Nobody knows.
Projects stall
Delayed cycles cost time and client trust
